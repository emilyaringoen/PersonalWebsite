About Me

Hello there! My name is Emily. I am a Colorado native who loves the mountains. I live in Boulder and spend my days with my boyfriend, my friends, and my adorable dog. The mountains provide an excellent escape from the real world--allowing me to ski, run, and rock climb in absolute beauty.

When I am not outside, you can find me reading, painting, or coding. I am striving to be a lifelong learner, and Galvanize is helping me along the way.
